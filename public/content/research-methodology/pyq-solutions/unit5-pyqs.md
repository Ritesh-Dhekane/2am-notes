## 1. Prepare a Research Outline (Standard Structure).
**[APR-2025 | JAN-2026 | 10 Marks]**

**Topic Example:** Role of AI in Education / Impact of Social Media.

### Standard Structure:
1. **Title:** Clear, concise, and descriptive.
2. **Abstract:** A 200-300 word summary of the entire study.
3. **Introduction:** Background of the study, problem statement, and significance.
4. **Literature Review:** Summary of existing work and identifying the research gap.
5. **Objectives:** What the research aims to achieve.
6. **Hypothesis:** Predictable statements to be tested ($H_0$ and $H_a$).
7. **Methodology:** Research design, sampling, and data collection methods.
8. **Results & Discussion:** Presentation of findings and their interpretation.
9. **Conclusion:** Summary of findings, limitations, and future scope.
10. **References:** List of all sources cited (APA/MLA style).

## 2. Case Study: Outline for "Impact of Social Media on Indian Youth"
**[APR-2025 | 10 Marks]**

* **Title:** A Study on the Psychological Impact of Instagram Usage on Indian Undergraduates.
* **Objectives:** 
    1. To measure the average time spent on social media daily.
    2. To analyze the correlation between social media usage and academic performance.
* **Hypothesis:** 
    * $H_0$: There is no significant impact of social media on academic scores.
    * $H_a$: High social media usage significantly reduces academic concentration.
* **Methodology:** 
    * **Sampling:** Stratified Random Sampling (10 colleges across India).
    * **Data Collection:** Online Questionnaire (Google Forms).
* **Research Gap:** Lack of longitudinal studies on Indian rural youth's social media habits.

## 3. What is a Research Report?
**[Short Note]**

A research report is a document that presents the findings of a research project. It is the final stage of the research process and serves as a formal record of the study. It must be written in an objective, clear, and professional language.
