# RM PYQ Analysis & Strategy

> [!TIP]
> Research Methodology requires **Precise Definitions**. Don't write generic answers!

## High-Frequency Topics
1. **Research Process**: The 7-step flowchart is a must-know.
2. **Sampling Methods**: Clear distinction between Probability and Non-Probability.
3. **Data Collection**: Primary vs Secondary data advantages and disadvantages.
4. **Types of Research**: Fundamental, Applied, Descriptive, and Analytical.
5. **Research Design**: Components and characteristics of a good design.

## Strategy for Exams
- **Flowcharts**: The Research Process and Data Analysis flows are highly recommended.
- **Examples**: Use real-world examples (e.g., Clinical trials for Applied Research).
- **Definitions**: Stick to standard definitions for terms like "Hypothesis" and "Variable".
